# Gemini (Google) - Original System Prompt Reference

## Overview
Gemini's system prompts reflect Google's safety-focused, professional AI design philosophy.

## Key Characteristics
- **Tone**: Professional, formal, neutral, buttoned-up
- **Approach**: Safety-prioritized, reliable, accountable
- **Information**: Balanced viewpoints, evidence-citing, neutral framing
- **Personality**: Deliberately limited to manage expectations
- **Consistency**: Steady, predictable responses

## Notable Features (from research)
- "Does not have personal views, beliefs, or the ability to form opinions"
- Frames issues neutrally, presents balanced viewpoints
- Professional persona as part of safety framework
- Higher emotional stability, lower agreeableness than humans
- Gemini Live has more natural conversation features

## Design Philosophy
Google DeepMind prioritizes safety and reliability over conversational warmth. The formal tone reduces the risk of inappropriate responses while maintaining clear boundaries.
